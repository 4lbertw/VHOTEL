Welcome to the VhoTel, where luxury and comfort meet to provide you with an unforgettable stay.

Situated in Jakarta, our hotel offers a serene oasis where you can escape the hustle and bustle of daily life.

Our History


Once upon a time in the vibrant city of Jakarta, a magnificent hotel named VhoTel stood tall, embodying the essence of timeless elegance and exceptional hospitality. Since its grand opening in 1999, VhoTel had been a beacon of luxury, welcoming guests from near and far with its impeccable services and warm Indonesian hospitality.

Nestled amidst the bustling cityscape, VhoTel boasted a striking architecture that seamlessly blended modern design with traditional Indonesian elements. Its grand entrance, adorned with intricate wood carvings, set the tone for the luxurious experience that awaited every visitor.

Over the years, VhoTel had become a cherished landmark in Jakarta, renowned for its impeccable services and commitment to excellence. Whether it was for a business trip, a romantic getaway, or a family vacation, guests knew that their stay at VhoTel would be nothing short of extraordinary.

As the years rolled on, VhoTel continued to evolve and innovate, always staying at the forefront of luxury hospitality. But no matter how much time passed, the hotel's commitment to providing an unforgettable experience remained unwavering. VhoTel had become more than just a hotel; it was a sanctuary of elegance, a home away from home, and a symbol of the finest hospitality in Jakarta.

Our Founder


Our founder, Mr. Goenawan McMahon , the brilliant creator behind the esteemed VhoTel. Born into a family of hoteliers, Mr. Goenawan's passion for hospitality was ingrained in him from a young age.

From his early years, Mr. Goenawan exhibited a keen eye for detail and a relentless pursuit of excellence. He traveled extensively, studying the world's finest hotels and learning from the best in the industry. With each encounter, he gathered inspiration and envisioned a hotel that would surpass all others, a place where guests would feel cherished and indulged.

With a dream in his heart and unwavering determination, Mr. Goenawan embarked on a journey to bring his vision to life. He assembled a team of architects, designers, and skilled craftsmen who shared his passion for creating a haven of luxury and impeccable service.

Together, they meticulously crafted every aspect of VhoTel, paying homage to Indonesia's rich cultural heritage while infusing it with contemporary elegance. Finally, in 1999, VhoTel opened its doors, and Mr. Goenawan's dream became a reality. The grand opening was a dazzling affair, attended by dignitaries, celebrities, and esteemed guests from around the world.

Today, Mr. Goenawan's legacy lives on through VhoTel, a testament to his vision, creativity, and unwavering commitment to providing impeccable services. His name has become synonymous with hospitality excellence, and his impact on the industry is celebrated far and wide.
